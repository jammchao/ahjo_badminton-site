About Me

I’m Joe, better known as AhJo Badminton, a passionate Malaysian badminton superfan, cheerleader, and digital content creator. My mission is twofold: to create an electrifying, unforgettable atmosphere directly on the stadium floors for our national athletes, and to capture that intense passion through high-energy digital content that unites millions of fans online.

To bridge the arena gap where Malaysian voices are often outnumbered, I am actively working to build a thriving online community and establish an official Malaysian badminton support and cheerleading team—boosting athlete morale and solidifying our badminton fan culture worldwide.

My Journey – Fueled by Love & Sacrifice

From local stadiums to international arenas, I travel directly to major world tournaments—bringing live stadium energy to both the stands and digital screens worldwide.
